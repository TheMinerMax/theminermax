Who are we?

Caringbah High School's Engineering Department is mix of multiple teachers, all coming from diverse engineering backgrounds. With over 100 years of combined experience, we can prepare students for jobs in the real world using engineering skills.

What we offer?

In year 7 and 8 students will do woodworking and coding, getting a taste of engineering. In years 9 and 10 students can take engineering as an elective, were they will engage in many different projects and aspects of engineering. We offer Engineering in Year 11 and 12, were students can further advance their skills and knowledge.

Why choose Engineering at Caringbah High School?

Engineering builds team work skills, and teaches students how to work in a team. It also teaches students how to solve problems and how to think outside the box.
